Continuing Education courses are further divided into four categories.

These are: (click a heading to see an explanation of each)

  • Courses
    • Courses typically require one hour to complete including post-test
    • Courses are designed as a quick way to learn more about a specific topic of interest
    • They require reading the selected online course material and taking a post-test, thereby keeping you up-to-date on the latest therapies, findings and happenings within your field of interest
    • Courses can be completed over your lunch hour or at home
  • Journal Clubs
    • Each Journal Club contains 2-5 peer-reviewed journal articles all pertaining to a relevant topic
    • Journal Clubs are a great way to keep up-to-date on the latest research and findings
    • Journal Clubs conclude with a post-test for CEU's
  • Lunch With The Experts
    • Lunch With The Experts are compiled from interviews, conference proceedings and recent literature bringing you a written composite of a particular expert's work
    • Each session allows you to spend some virtual time with a leading expert--get to know their work firsthand!
    • Each is followed by a post-test allowing you to collect CEU's
  • Conference Reviews
    • Conference Reviews allow you to virtually go to conferences you are unable to physically attend!
    • Each conference is brought to you with speaker reviews and seminar notes--even photos!
    • Each is followed by a post-test in order to earn CEU's
