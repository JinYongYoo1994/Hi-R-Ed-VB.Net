Accreditations

All courses are accredited for nurses, case managers, social workers and registered dietitians. All courses are always free. Depending on the course, after successfully completing each course's post-test, CE certificates will be immediately available for printing , or will be delivered to your email within two business days.

Nurses:
Hi-R-Ed Online University is approved as a provider of continuing education by the California Board of Registered Nursing (CEP 13804). The programs listed on the course selection page will earn the participant continuing education credit upon successful completion of the post-test. Courses vary in the number of credits available.

Social Workers:

Hi-R-Ed Online University, provider #1091, is approved to offer social work continuing education by the Association of Social Work Boards (ASWB) Approved Continuing Education (ACE) program. Organizations, not individual courses, are approved as ACE providers. State and provincial regulatory boards have the final authority to determine whether an individual course may be accepted for continuing education credit. Hi-R-Ed Online University maintains responsibility for these courses. ACE provider approval period: 2/28/19 - 2/28/22.

 

Registered Dietitians:

Hi-R-Ed Online University is an approved provider with the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics through their Commission on Dietetic Registration (provider #NU003).

 

Certified Case Managers:

Some courses on this site are approved by the Commission for Case Manager Certification (CCMC) for continuing education credit for Certified Case Managers. Sign In to take a course to see details.
