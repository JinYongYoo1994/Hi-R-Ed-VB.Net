   Instructions

  1. From the Continuing Education home page, click on the link that best represents your profession. This will take you to the registration page. 
  2. New Users must fill out the registration form. Returning Users may click on the “Returning User” link to bypass the registration screen*. You will not receive CE credit if you do not register.                               
  3. At the Course Selection page click on the course of your choice.
  4. When you click on a course title you will be taken immediately to the first page of the course, unless this course requires a user name and password.
    Follow the on-screen prompts throughout the course. At the conclusion of each course is an evaluation and post-test.
    The evaluation and post-test must be completed with a post-test score of 80% or above for continuing education credits to be awarded.
